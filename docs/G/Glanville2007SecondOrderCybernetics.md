# Definitions / terms this changes (only the ones that matter)
- **Second-order cybernetics:** A reflexive stance where the observer is explicitly included; inquiry targets the observing system, not a supposedly observer-independent object. ``[@Glanville2007SecondOrderCybernetics, p. 1–3]``
- **Black Box:** An observer-inserted explanatory construct that enables action while remaining essentially opaque; its “mechanism” is a product of interaction and modelling. ``[@Glanville2007SecondOrderCybernetics, p. 4–5]``
- **Circularity (as method):** Not “feedback as control hierarchy,” but mutual causality and betweenness; stability emerges from relations, not isolated parts. ``[@Glanville2007SecondOrderCybernetics, p. 3–6]``
- **Ethics (as method property):** Responsibility belongs to the observer; ethical action increases choices (design generosity as a parallel). ``[@Glanville2007SecondOrderCybernetics, p. 11–12]``
